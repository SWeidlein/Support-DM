Why?

Creating a support case is sometimes critical in ensuring our customer promise is kept however creating one in scenarios that don't require it can impact our response time. In order to ensure this doesn't occur it's our mission to provide the appropriate training and resources.

When Not To

While on call with a driver there are issues that you are bound to not know how to approah. Please be advised in those situations that is completely normal and there is a whole team ready to help you. Feel free to contact S&D, team leads, trainers, or even your peers.If there is a scenario where a driver requires:


  • Empty Trailer Cancellation

  • ITR

  • Performance related disputes

  • Unable to complete Tour

    • Please proceed with the approapiate standard work for all provided examples. C2C has authorization to cancel FleetManagementEquipmentRepositioning and TrailorPoolAdjustment. If driver is stating a CF or non-CF load is pending cancellation please verify with site personnel or request for a case to be created on the sites end. All ITR or TA concerns should be submitted through sim tickets if there is any questions or concerns please reach out to recovery. If a driver is unable to complete a tour whether it be due to personal,overbooking, or mechanical issues please reject back to loadboard. ROC does not handle performance or payment related concerns if there is any issue advise carrier to submit a dispute through their relay app.
